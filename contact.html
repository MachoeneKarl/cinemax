Common Questions

QUICK ANSWERS

How do I book a ticket?
Visit our Booking page, select your movie, choose your seats and showtime, then pay securely. You'll get an instant receipt.
How quickly will you reply?
We respond to all messages within 24 hours, Monday to Sunday. Urgent matters can be sent directly to our email.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are available up to 2 hours before showtime. Select "Booking" as your inquiry type and include your booking reference.
Do you offer group bookings?
Yes! Groups of 10+ receive discounted rates. Use the "Quote" inquiry type with your group size and preferred date.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major debit and credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), as well as pay-at-venue for in-person bookings.
Is my card information safe?
Yes. All payments are processed with SSL encryption. We do not store any card details on our servers.
